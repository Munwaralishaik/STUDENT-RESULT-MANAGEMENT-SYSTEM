About Student Result Management System

Welcome to our Student Result Management System! Our system is designed to efficiently manage student results, making it easier for both students and faculty to access and track academic progress.

Features:

  • Secure login system for students and faculty.
  • Ability for students to view their results and academic history.
  • Faculty can enter, update, and manage student grades.
  • Automated calculation of GPA and other academic metrics.
  • Generate and download official transcripts.
  • Notification system for important updates and announcements.
